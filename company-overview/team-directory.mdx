## Leadership

**Dan Schuleman — CEO & Co-Founder**

Dan is passionate about creating novel workflow automation and knowledge solutions for insurance professionals inspired by his legal career. Prior to founding Qumis, Dan was Associate General Counsel at Kin Insurance, a Chicago-based unicorn direct-to-consumer homeowners insurance company. He holds a J.D. from the University of Illinois College of Law and a B.A. with departmental honors from Northwestern University.

**Shivam Sinha — CTO & Co-Founder**

Shiv is a proven technology leader with 15+ years of experience within financial services. His last corporate role was Head of Application Development at Goldman Sachs where he launched Marcus.com, a multi-billion dollar consumer deposits business. He was previously Co-Founder and CTO of Newtrul, a venture capital backed logistics startup in Chicago.
