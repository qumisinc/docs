Qumis is an assistive-AI platform that can quickly analyze insurance policies and pull relevant information for claims, brokering, and compliance. Developed by insurance attorneys, Qumis delivers law firm grade analysis and enables real-time discussions with your policies and institutional knowledge, transforming hours of work into minutes with enhanced precision.

Qumis leverages cutting-edge technology to extract relevant information for claims, brokering, and compliance, delivering projected hard dollar ROI of up to 400% to our customers. Our product suite deeply resonates with our customers and it is only the beginning.

Founded in 2022, Qumis has participated in multiple highly selective insurtech accelerator programs including:

- **Fintech Innovation Lab New York** — top 3% of applicants
- **BrokerTech Ventures accelerator** — top 9% of applicants
- **Creative Destruction Lab – Risk accelerator** — top 7% of applicants
