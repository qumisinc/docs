The official company handbook can be found in Rippling. The following is general information about day-to-day operations and company policies.

## Office environment

- Qumis operates on a hybrid schedule. Our teams work in-person at the company offices on **Mondays and Thursdays**.
- To access the office, you will use your WeWork card and the key provided on your first day. If you lose your keys or card, please reach out to **Hiral Lakhani**.
- We empower our employees to do their best work by providing flexibility on when they start and end their day. The goal is always to produce your best work in a timely manner. On in-office days, employees are expected to be in the office for a majority of business hours.

## Expense policy & management

Submit all expense receipts to [expenses@rippling.com](mailto:expenses@rippling.com).

### Overtime

Employees working past 8:00 PM at the office may expense a meal and an Uber home.

### Travel

- **Flights & Hotel:** Book via Ramp to access our discounted rates.
- **Meals:**
  - Breakfast & Lunch: Up to $25
  - Dinner: Up to $50

### Home office stipend

All full-time employees receive a **$1,000 stipend** to set up their home office.

## Holidays & PTO

- Qumis has an **unlimited PTO policy**. We ask that you submit all time-off requests through Rippling for your manager's visibility.
- Please mark your calendar when taking PTO to ensure team members are aware of your availability.

## Internal onboarding checklist

### Before day 1

- [ ] Ensure their manager has coordinated first day arrival logistics
- [ ] Add the employee to the WeWork Admin Portal
- [ ] Ensure they have a workspace and desk setup
- [ ] Assign an onboarding buddy
- [ ] Create/verify Rippling account and onboarding tasks
- [ ] Provision tool access (Gmail, Slack, Zoom, Google Drive, Vanta)
- [ ] Send Day 1 calendar invite

### Day 1

- [ ] Schedule and hold welcome meeting with the new employee
- [ ] Add the new employee to all company-wide meetings
- [ ] Add to relevant Slack channels
- [ ] Walk through [docs.qumis.ai](https://docs.qumis.ai)
