## Contributing

If you're working in customer support and have prompts that have proven valuable, please contribute them to this library:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Test your prompt">
    Use the prompt in real customer support scenarios to validate its effectiveness
  </Step>

  <Step title="Document the use case">
    Note when and how to use the prompt, including any prerequisites or best practices
  </Step>

  <Step title="Share with the team">
    Add your prompt to this page following the format of other prompt category pages
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  **Suggested Prompt Categories for Customer Support:**
  - Email response templates
  - Issue triage and categorization
  - Knowledge base article generation
  - Customer communication tone adjustment
  - Bug report summarization
</Tip>
